About Cathrich

Building partnerships across borders since 1997

Cathrich No.13cc is a well-established provider of procurement, import, and export services, supporting clients across South Africa and Mozambique for over 27 years.

Building on our success in Mozambique, we have recently expanded our operations into additional neighboring African markets, including Botswana, Namibia, Tanzania, and Zimbabwe.

Our business is built on reliability, operational efficiency, and long-term partnerships. We deliver end-to-end procurement and logistics solutions tailored to meet the specific requirements of each client.

The company is privately owned and directed by Lynette Lindsay, who has led Cathrich No.13cc since its inception in 1997. Under her leadership, the company has developed a strong reputation for integrity, accountability, and service excellence.
